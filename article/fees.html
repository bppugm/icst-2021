Conference fees

Due to the extraordinary global health issue with the coronavirus disease (COVID-19), ICST 2020 will be held virtually on 7–8 September 2020.
Please note that participation in the ICST 2020 is subject to several terms and conditions.
Participation type Early bird discount (until 14 July) Regular Additional paper(s)
Presenter
Foreign US$150 US$175 US$150
Local Rp1,500,000 Rp1,750,000 Rp1,500,000
Non-presenter is free of charge.

Terms and conditions

  1. The registration fee covers seminar materials, attendance of all technical sessions, and publication.
  2. The registration fee covers one participant only. Multiple authors who are attending must register separately, and those who are not presenting should register as a non-presenter.
  3. A non-presenter participant can attend two selected sessions only and won't get any seminar materials. Each session has limited participants.
  4. A receipt will be generated from the payment confirmation details after the your payment is verified.
  5. Payments are nonrefundable.
  6. Payments cannot be made in cash; they must be via e-billing or credit card.
  7. Participants are responsible for transfer fees and bank charges.
  8. At least one author for each accepted paper must register as a presenter.
  9. Participants are charged based on their institution/affiliation not the nationality of the first author.
  10. Submitting an extra paper is applicable only to the same presenter.
  11. The early bird discount will be expired at July 14, 23:59 am (GMT+7).
