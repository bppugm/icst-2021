How can we help you?

  icst@ugm.ac.id   +62 858-3491-4517 (Nafi)

  1. If you want to submit a manuscript, you need to click the  Participate button.
  2. Select  Presenter and click the  Participate here button in the symposium that you wanted.
  3. You wil directed to symposium page that informs the completed topics, committee, author guidelines, and paper submission steps.
  4. Read all the information carefully.
  5. Follow the author guidelines and all the paper submission steps.

  • If you create a new EDAS account, you need to send an email to help@edas.info and fill the activation form in the symposium page. This activation need a day or more because EDAS team will activate it manually.
  • If you forgot your password, you can reset it from EDAS page by clicking the icon .

  • If you already have a Scientific.Net account, you need to request an account activation in the  Paper Submission tab from the Life Sciences, Materials, and Applied Chemistry symposium page . Click  Activate your account tab and fill the activation request form. This activation need a day or more because the administrator will activate it manually. If you still require further assistance, please feel free to contact us.
  • If you forgot your password, you can reset it from Scientific.Net page and enter your registered email address.
